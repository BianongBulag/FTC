Binondo, Manila

Oct 02, 2026  ·  6 min read

The Ultimate Binondo Crawl

The oldest Chinatown in the world offers an overwhelming sensory experience. Escaping the sweltering Manila heat meant ducking into tiny hole-in-the-wall establishments to find comfort in a bowl of hand-pulled noodles.

What I ate

  • Fried Siopao — Shanghai Fried Siopao
  • Hand-pulled beef noodles — Lan Zhou La Mien
  • Toasted Siopao near the church

There's an unnamed cart selling the crispiest lumpia at 2 PM exactly. Get there by 1:45.

Osaka, Japan

Sep 15, 2026  ·  8 min read

Kuidaore in Dotonbori

"Kuidaore" — to eat oneself into ruin. The neon lights reflect off the canal while the rich smell of okonomiyaki and takoyaki wafts through the chilly air. It was a chaotic, delicious weekend.

What I ate

  • Michelin-starred takoyaki
  • Wagyu beef skewers
  • Melon pan ice cream

Skip the main chains. Head to the narrow alleys behind Dotombori arcades — shorter wait, chefs griddle right in front of you.
